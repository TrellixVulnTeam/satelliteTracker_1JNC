About The Satellite Tracker

Controls:

Click and Drag to Move the Globe Around

Press Space Bar to Start/Stop Planet Rotation

Click on a Satellite or GroundSite to Turn the Element on or off

This project tracks 100 different satellites in their orbits around the earth, with 15 groundsites on the earth's surface. The point of the project is to visualize satellite paths in 3D, while also showing where those paths are visible to different points on the earth.

The application currently allows for the user to select and view satellite orbits. The satellite path shown is a 24 hour snapshot of the satellite's future path. The user can also select a ground site to show where the satellite's orbit will be visible to that location on the earth.

Some of the orbits will look a little crazy, which is expected. I'm not showing a traditional ellipse of a satellite's path. Because of that, I've made sure to verify the orbital positions with n2yo.com, and everything has matched up correctly.
