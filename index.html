About The Satellite Tracker

Controls:

Click and Drag to Move the Globe Around

Press Space Bar to Start/Stop Planet Rotation

Click on a Satellite or GroundSite to Turn the Element on or off

This project is an experiment in trying to track satellites across the sky, and show where they are visible to different points on the earth. The path shown for each satellite is a 24 hour period calculated from TLE (Two Line Element) data gathered from space-track.org.

Some of the orbits will look a little crazy, as I'm not showing a traditional ellipse of a satellite's path. I've verified the satellite orbits this project shows with n2yo.com, and the data matches up.
